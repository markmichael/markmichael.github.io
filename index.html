My Projects

Project ScanCap

My newest project still in it's fledgling stages. By June 2017, our team will design and build a capsule endoscope to screen for esophageal cancer in low-resource settings.

Instant Raman

Pap smears are useful for screening for cervical cancer, but they are not feasible in rural parts of developing countries. We designed an affordable Raman spectrometer to better meet the screening needs of these populations.

Ebola EMR

A simple disease-specific electronic medical record system designed to effeciently record and recall patient information in an outbreak-type scenario.
